> Everything you need to know about how our server is organized — from staff roles to the bots that keep things running smoothly.

## Staff Roles

- **Owner** — Reviews high-level appeals, manages infrastructure, and sets the direction of the community
- **Admin** — Oversees the moderation team and maintains bots/services
- **Moderator** — Enforces rules, handles tickets, and keeps the community safe
- **Helper** — Community members recognized for their technical expertise and willingness to help others throughout the server

---

## Community Roles

- **Contributor** — Awarded to those who have contributed code to our open-source projects
- **Booster** — A cosmetic role for those who boost the server
- **Member** — The heart of our community!

---

## Bots

- **@CodeVerseBot** — Our custom bot handling utility and simple moderation tasks
- **@ModMail** — Send a DM to this bot to open a private ticket with staff

> Need help with a bot? Ask in the appropriate support channel or check the pinned messages.
