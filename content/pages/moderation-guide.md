> Our moderation team is committed to keeping the community safe. If you see a violation, please report it — your help makes our community better.

## Reporting Violations

To report a rule violation:

1. **Open a ModMail ticket** — DM the @ModMail bot with details of the incident
2. **Provide evidence** — Include screenshots, message links, or any relevant context
3. **Be respectful** — Keep your report factual and avoid emotional language

> All reports are treated confidentially. You will never be penalized for making a good-faith report.

---

## Handling Reports

Moderators should:

1. **Review the evidence** — Carefully examine the provided information
2. **Consult the rules** — Ensure the reported behavior clearly violates our rules
3. **Take action** — Issue warnings, temporary bans, or permanent bans as appropriate
4. **Document actions** — Keep records of all moderation actions for future reference

---

## Appeals Process

Users may appeal moderation actions by:

1. **Opening a ModMail ticket** — Explain why they believe the action was unjust
2. **Providing context** — Include any relevant information or evidence
3. **Waiting for review** — The moderation team will review the appeal and respond accordingly

> We strive to be fair and transparent in all moderation decisions. If you feel you've been treated unfairly, we encourage you to appeal.
